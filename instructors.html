Instructors


Beth Krenning


Beth is a certified NRA Instructor whose focus is on keeping the 2nd Amendment*. She teaches how to use and care for guns for sport and defense. One of her main goals is to have safe firearms instruction for the entire family. Beth teaches in Boise, Meridian, Caldwell, Nampa and the surrounding area.

Certifications:

  • NRA Pistol Instructor
  • NRA Rifle Instructor
  • NRA Personal Protection inside the Home Instructor
  • NRA Personal Protection outside the Home Instructor

* 2nd Amendment - A well regulated militia being necessary to the security of a free state, the right of the people to keep and bear arms shall not be infringed.
